# 🐞 Bug Report – Messenger Video Call

## 📌 Description
When a user is on a video call and switches the call to another device using the same account,
the camera turns off automatically after the switch.

## 🔍 Steps to Reproduce
1. Open Messenger on Device A
2. Start a video call with another user
3. On Device B (same account), initiate or take over the call (switch device)
4. Observe the camera after switching

## ❌ Actual Result
The camera turns off after switching to the second devices

## ✅ Expected Result
The camera should remain active or automatically resume after switching devices

## 💡 Workaround
After switching the call to another device, the user can manually turn off
the camera and turn it back on again to restore video.

## ⚠️ Severity / Priority
- Severity: Medium
- Priority: Medium

## 🛠 Environment
- Device: iPhone
- OS: iOS
- app: Messenger
